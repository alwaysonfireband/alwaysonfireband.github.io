Your musical journey through the ages

From the iconic riffs of Queen to the rebellious beats of Muse, from the unmistakable groove of ZZ Top to the soulful melodies of Joe Walsh - "Always on Fire" is your musical time machine to classic rock's golden age.

Consisting of five passionate musicians from Chicago, IL, our ensemble brings together a symphony of sounds, echoing the sentiments of yesteryears while infusing our unique touch. We are not just a band; we're a bridge that connects generations of rock enthusiasts.

With each performance, we aim to capture the essence of classic rock, transporting audiences to a time where music wasn't just heard; it was felt. Our sets promise not only a trip down memory lane but a live experience that resonates with both seasoned classic rock aficionados and new fans alike.

Join us on this sonic journey, as we pay homage to legends of rock, celebrating their legacy with every chord and chorus.
